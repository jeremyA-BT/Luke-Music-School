About Luke

Professional musician and dedicated educator specializing in piano, guitar, drums, vocals, bass, and ukulele.

With years of experience in both performance and education, Luke brings a unique blend of technical expertise and passionate teaching to every lesson. His approach combines classical foundations with contemporary techniques, ensuring students develop both skill and artistic expression.

Luke's diverse musical background spans multiple genres and instruments, making him an ideal instructor for students of all ages and skill levels. From beginners taking their first steps into music to advanced players looking to refine their craft, Luke creates personalized learning experiences that inspire and challenge.
